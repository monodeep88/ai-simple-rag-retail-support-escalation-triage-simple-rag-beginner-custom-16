Customer Returns Policy v2.1

1.  **Standard Returns:** Items can be returned within 30 days of purchase with a valid receipt for a full refund to the original payment method. Item must be in new, resalable condition.
2.  **Returns Without Receipt:** For items returned without a receipt, store credit will be issued based on the item's lowest selling price in the last 60 days. Manager override is required for any cash refund without a receipt or for items valued over $50.
3.  **Defective Items:** Defective items can be exchanged or refunded within 90 days. If the defect is due to customer misuse, refund may be denied. Manager approval required for denial.
4.  **Final Sale Items:** All items marked 'Final Sale' are non-returnable and non-refundable.
